About Yomalis Rosario

Yomalis Rosario (she/her) was born & raised in Washington Heights, NYC. She currently lives in Newark, NJ with her husband and two children.

Professional Experience

Yomalis started her career as a public school teacher. She studied History at Dartmouth College and has a Master's in Teaching from the University of Washington. Now, she is a full-time parent, part-time Workshop Administrator for Brooklyn Poets, and teaching artist with a renewed devotion to poetry & creativity. She also has experience as a Curriculum Designer, Assessment Editor, Manuscript Editor, Programs Manager, and Barista.

Creative Work

Yomalis is a poet who recently graduated from the Brooklyn Poets Mentorship Program. Her work appears or is forthcoming in The Poetry Lab, Epiphany Magazine, ROOM Magazine, Nimrod, and elsewhere. Her work has been supported by Brooklyn Poets and the Martha's Vineyard Institute of Creative Writing. Yomalis is currently working on her first poetry manuscript.

Yomalis is also the Founder and Managing Editor of WE THE SOIL, an online literary magazine that celebrates creative community and aims to bring much-needed warmth and connection to the world of literary publishing. In order to support the work of teaching artists and the magic of writers gathering to create together, she also started a free monthly newsletter that shares upcoming workshops as well as resources and opportunities for writers & artists. She frequently hosts free/donation-based virtual creativity circle and study circles.
